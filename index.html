Freicoin: a p2p cash currency with Silvio Gesell's demurrage

Freicoin is a peer-to-peer (p2p) currency based on the cryptographic concept of a proof-of-work block chain conceived by Satoshi Nakamoto, the original developer of Bitcoin.

Unlike Bitcoin, Freicoin has a demurrage) fee that ensures its circulation and bearers of the currency pay this fee automatically. This demurrage fee was proposed by Silvio Gesell and has been tested several times with positive results.
